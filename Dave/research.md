## Market Landscape

### Consulting Industry (2025-2026)
- AI, data analytics, and automation present significant consulting opportunities — many businesses claim to be data-driven but transformation remains slow
- 2026 trend: short diagnostics combined with targeted implementation, hybrid/remote teams to reduce cost and increase flexibility
- Smaller, specialized firms and solo practitioners are gaining ground vs. large consultancies
- Outcome-based and fixed-fee pricing increasingly preferred over time-and-materials

### Fractional Executive Market
- 52.8% of fractional leaders earned over $100K in 2024
- 72.8% have 15+ years of experience — this is a veteran's game
- Highest demand in tech, SaaS, healthcare, and **manufacturing**
- Industry 4.0 transformation (IoT, AI, robotics, data analytics) creating demand for fractional operations leaders
- Fractional Chief Quality Officer is an established role (platforms like GigX, FactoryQA list it specifically)
- Manufacturing Extension Partnerships (e.g., WMEP) actively offer fractional services for manufacturers with lean teams

### Caterpillar Context
- CAT has been conducting layoffs across multiple divisions (2025-2026), including engineering and quality roles
- Outsourcing to low-cost countries (India, China) while reducing domestic headcount
- Plant closures (e.g., Wamego, Kansas — ~400 employees)
- RTO mandates used as headcount reduction strategy
- This creates a pool of experienced QI professionals entering the market — differentiation matters

### Quality Profession Demand
- Manufacturing undergoing "renaissance" driven by automation, robotics, Industry 4.0
- Strong demand for professionals who can integrate AI with established methods (SPC, Lean, Six Sigma)
- Reshoring and domestic production increases driving supply chain quality needs
- Adjacent high-demand sectors: life sciences/medical devices (FDA regulations), healthcare (predictive quality intelligence), aerospace (AS9100)

## Business Model Options

### 1. Solo Quality Consulting
- **What:** Project-based or retainer engagements — process optimization, QMS implementation, data analysis, strategic QI guidance
- **Startup cost:** $10K-$25K (home-based: legal, branding, software, initial marketing)
- **Time to revenue:** 1-3 months with existing network; 3-6 months cold
- **Rates:** $150-$300/hr for experienced manufacturing QI consultants; project fees $5K-$50K+ depending on scope
- **Scalability:** Limited by personal hours; can grow to small firm over time

### 2. Fractional Quality Officer
- **What:** Part-time senior quality leadership for multiple SMBs — 10-20 hrs/week per client
- **Startup cost:** Same as consulting (~$10K-$25K)
- **Time to revenue:** 1-3 months via platforms and network
- **Rates:** $5K-$15K/month per client retainer (2-4 clients = strong income)
- **Scalability:** 2-4 concurrent clients; can hire junior support

### 3. ISO Certification Consulting
- **What:** Guide small/mid manufacturers through ISO 9001, AS9100, ISO 13485 certification
- **Startup cost:** $10K-$20K + lead auditor certification if not already held
- **Time to revenue:** 2-4 months
- **Rates:** $10K-$50K per certification project (6-12 month engagements typical)
- **Scalability:** Repeatable, well-defined scope; can productize with templates/checklists
- **Note:** 100% success rate claims common among established ISO consultancies — high-value, low-risk for clients

### 4. QI Training & Certification Programs
- **What:** Lean Six Sigma training (Yellow/Green/Black Belt), SPC workshops, quality analytics courses
- **Startup cost:** $5K-$15K (curriculum development, online platform, marketing)
- **Time to revenue:** 3-6 months to build and market
- **Rates:** $500-$5,500 per participant depending on depth; corporate training $2K-$15K per engagement
- **Scalability:** Highly scalable once content is built — online delivery, corporate contracts

### 5. Quality Analytics SaaS (Micro-SaaS)
- **What:** Niche software tool for specific QI pain point (e.g., SPC dashboards, supplier quality scoring, audit management)
- **Startup cost:** $50K-$150K+ (development, infrastructure); lower with no-code/low-code approach
- **Time to revenue:** 6-18 months
- **Rates:** $200-$2,000/month subscription per client
- **Scalability:** Highest ceiling but highest risk and longest runway

### 6. AI + Quality Niche Consulting
- **What:** Help manufacturers integrate AI/ML into quality processes — predictive maintenance, anomaly detection, automated inspection
- **Startup cost:** $15K-$30K (additional training/tools)
- **Time to revenue:** 3-6 months
- **Rates:** Premium — $200-$400/hr; project fees $20K-$100K+
- **Scalability:** High demand, limited supply of practitioners who understand both QI and AI
