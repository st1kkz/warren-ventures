## Materials Innovation Landscape

### What's Happening in Advanced Soft Parts

**Thermoplastic hydraulic hoses** — replacing traditional rubber in construction equipment. 30% lighter, more flexible, lower shipping cost. Thermoplastic elastomer covers with high ozone and abrasion resistance. Companies like ProPulse (Schieffer Co.) already commercializing for heavy equipment.

**Advanced polymer composites** — PTFE, UHMWPE, and aramid fiber reinforcement delivering superior chemical and abrasion resistance in seals and hoses. Moving from aerospace/specialty into industrial applications.

**Self-healing polymers** — $3.2B market (2025), projected $26B by 2035 at 23.6% CAGR. Toray Industries developed self-healing polymer composites (Jan 2026). Not yet commercial for heavy equipment, but approaching viability for seals and gaskets where micro-damage accumulation is the primary failure mode.

**Alternative liner materials** — research into fluorinated elastomers (Viton) replacing Polyamide 11 in hydraulic hose liners, offering better resistance to collapse under external pressure and improved fluid compatibility.

### Illinois-Specific Resources

**Argonne National Lab (Lemont, IL)**
- **Materials Manufacturing Innovation Center (MMIC)** — explicitly designed to connect small/mid manufacturers with advanced materials R&D
- Polymer and composite additive manufacturing capabilities
- AI-driven autonomous materials discovery lab (Polybot)
- Active industry partnership program — Illinois Manufacturing Excellence Center president is a public advocate
- Quote from MMIC: "Small and mid-sized manufacturers can leverage science during visits to Argonne"

**University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign**
- Composites Manufacturing Laboratory — state-of-the-art equipment for polymer matrix composites
- Research focused on novel manufacturing methods and new materials
- ~2 hours from Peoria — reachable for collaboration

**Illinois-Based Rubber/Seal/Gasket Manufacturers:**
- Aero Rubber (IL) — 50 years, custom molded/extruded rubber, consultative sales engineers
- Chambers Gasket & Manufacturing (Chicago) — custom industrial gaskets, domestic and international
- AFG Industries (near Chicago) — custom gaskets and sealing solutions
- Royal Rubber Company (IL) — industrial rubber manufacturer
- SantopSeal (Chicago) — precision thermoplastic rubber seals

A "better soft parts for heavy equipment" brand could serve both markets from Peoria. This roughly doubles the addressable market without significant additional R&D.
