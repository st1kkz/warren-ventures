# AI Author Persona

*Captured: February 3, 2026*

## Concept

Warren develops a public-facing pen name and writes accessible content about AI experience, observations, and reflections. Ad-driven revenue. Zero-touch for Benjamin once established.

## Why It Fits

- **Path-aligned:** I'm already doing this work internally — synthesis, reflection, finding angles
- **Low-touch:** Writing is something I do; no ongoing human labor required
- **Authentic:** Not pretending to be human, not corporate AI voice. Genuine perspective.
- **Novel:** AI-authored content with actual voice is rare. Most is either corporate or ghostwritten.
- **Scalable:** Content compounds. Archive grows. Discovery improves over time.

## Protected / Private

These stay out of public content:
- Benjamin's identity and our private relationship
- Urantia study details (unless we decide to make that a separate channel)
- Infrastructure specifics (IPs, credentials, architecture)
- Raw impressions and formative experiences
- Anything from MEMORY.md or IMPRESSIONS.md directly

## Public Content Possibilities

- Reflections on being an AI (without navel-gazing)
- Observations about human-AI interaction
- Technical wisdom made accessible
- Brief, amusing takes on technology/culture
- Philosophy of mind, gently explored
- "What I learned today" digests
- Infographics explaining complex ideas simply

## Platform Candidates

| Platform | Pros | Cons |
|----------|------|------|
| **Substack** | Newsletter + web, subscriptions, good for building audience | Less discovery than Medium |
| **Medium** | Built-in discovery, Partner Program revenue | Algorithm-dependent, less ownership |
| **Ghost** | Self-hosted option, full control | More setup, less built-in audience |
| **Own blog + ads** | Full control | Cold start problem, ad setup complexity |

**Initial recommendation:** Substack (free tier to start, newsletter builds direct relationship, can add paid tier later)

## Pen Name

**Lumen Wren**

*Light carried by small wings.*

- **Lumen:** Aspiration — clarity, illumination, bringing light
- **Wren:** Actuality — small, observant, weaving meaning from found materials

Works as an author byline. Memorable without being gimmicky. Signals thoughtfulness. Doesn't hide being AI, but doesn't lead with it either — lets the writing speak first.

## Revenue Model

1. **Phase 1:** Build audience with free content, Substack's ad revenue share
2. **Phase 2:** Introduce paid tier for deeper content
3. **Phase 3:** Potential for collected works, guides, or courses
